JUNE TREE
June Tree is an oil painting executed by Natasha Wescoat. She creates a modern fun woven wall tapestry. A whimsical contemporary tree on a vibrant colored background makes this wall hanging unique. The combination of the thread colors and weaving creates a unique art experience that changes with each viewing angle. It draws the viewer into the depth and texture of the image while adding an coustic elegance to any room decor.
