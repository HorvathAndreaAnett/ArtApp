LA MUSE
La Muse painting, also known as Young Woman Drawing and Two Women, was painted in 1935, surrealism and cubism were a common synthesis, with alive and warm colors. Picasso did a series of paintings of young women drawing, writing or reading. This is Marie Therese Walther, not with the rounded, ample forms the painter normally used to depict her but with an angular style. The sleeping girl resembles her as well, and indeed she would be somewhere in Picasso's affection.

It is one of the famous paintings of this artist. It reflects the perspective of the artist in terms of technique used. The same color and formality are encountered in different works. The colors, the perspective and depth leave the artist's footsteps to the imagination world.
